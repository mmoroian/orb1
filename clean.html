Appendix – On Moving Day:

  • It is recommended to have your children away from your residence for safety reasons. If you can’t, please designate a babysitter for your kids during the packing / loading process.
  • Put your pets in a room where they will not be in the way of the moving process.
  • Keep all your belongings that are not going in one designated locked area. This will insure that items that need to be shipped will go and items that need to stay, stay!
  • Keep all jewellery and cash away from your home on this day. The moving process is relatively fast, and you may not be able to keep track of where you place them.
  • If you are moving during the winter, make sure that the whole access area from the truck / container to inside your residence is clean of snow and ice. Also, please put salt on the driveway and entryway – it can get very slippery.
  • "Work" with us and be on hand when we arrive. If you are not able to be present while the movers are there, designate a friend or family member to help and make decisions in your absence. Let us know to whom you have given the authority. Be sure that your representative knows exactly what is going and what is staying. This person may be asked to sign documents.
  • Stay home until the last item is packed. Make a “final inspection” before we leave. Check the inventory list and make sure you agree with any notations on it. Take pictures if necessary. It is your responsibility to verify that all of your goods are loaded.
  • Leave your phone connected throughout moving day. After the movers leave, pack your phone in one of your suitcases for easy access when you arrive to your new home. Ask us about electrical transformers and adapters!
  • You will be asked to provide us with all your contact information at your new residence and where you can be reached while your shipment is in transit.
